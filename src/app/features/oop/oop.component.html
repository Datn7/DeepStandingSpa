🧠 What Does This All Mean?

Imagine Shape is a blueprint — like a document saying "every shape must have a name and a way to calculate area". But it doesn’t know how to do it.

Circle and Square take that blueprint and actually implement the logic differently. This is called inheritance and polymorphism.

Encapsulation protects the data — just like your car’s engine is hidden and you control it with pedals. And abstraction means you can’t use the blueprint (Shape) directly — you must use a concrete version (like Circle).
